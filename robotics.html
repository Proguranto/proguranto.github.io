Description

  • Position: Software sub-system, Autonomous Navigation member
  • Current Project: Implementing DGNSS to the rover's Navigation system

    • Husky Robotics was the very first RSO I joined in UW as I am interested in exploring different fields of software and building mars rovers piqued my interest. So far, my time in Husky Robotics has been amazing, I got to meet several passionate team members and amazing individuals in general. As of now, I chose to become a part of the Autonomous Navigation team, in the software sub-system and cooperating with my peers in researching and implementing a DGNSS system in our rover. I think that Husky Robotics will definitely give me some experience in a field of Computer Science, which my study interest, and definitely impace what career field I wish to pursue, as I was able to learn how I loved doing the math and implementing it in code. I am hoping to be a part of Husky Robotics for meantime and hope that I will be able to experience a wonderful journey here!!
