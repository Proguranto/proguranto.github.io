Husky Robotics

First RSO I joined and we get to build mars rovers!!!

Description

  • Position: Software sub-system, Autonomous Navigation member
  • Current Project: Implementing DGNSS to the rover's Navigation system

    • Husky Robotics was the very first RSO I joined in UW as I am interested in exploring different fields of software and building mars rovers piqued my interest. So far, my time in Husky Robotics has been amazing, I got to meet several passionate team members and amazing individuals in general. As of now, I am a part of the Autonomous Navigation team, in the software sub-system and working alongside my peers in researching and implementing a DGNSS system in our rover. Husky Robotics has given me a new perspective on Computer Science where it impacted the my career field interest as I discover while doing research on how I am interested in the math behind theses systems as well as its implementations. I am planning to still be a part of Husky Robotics in the future and hope that I will be able to experience a wonderful journey here!!
